# Love, Violeta Rose Wax Seal Kit

## 1. Overview

A **premium, handcrafted wax seal stamping kit** designed as a supporter item and brand artifact for Love, Violeta Rose. This luxury stationery product embodies the brand's aesthetic of timeless romance, artisanal craftsmanship, and editorial elegance.

The kit serves multiple business purposes:
- **Revenue diversification** beyond wedding film services
- **Brand engagement** for fans, past clients, and supporters
- **Marketing artifact** that reinforces luxury positioning
- **Gift market** for wedding-adjacent purchases (save-the-dates, invitations, thank-you cards)

### What's Included

**Core Kit Components:**
- **Rose-engraved brass stamp head** (custom LVR design)
- **Wooden handle** (natural or branded finish)
- **Wax beads** (rose-wax-red #A14C41 and warm-sand #E9DDD2 colors)
- **Melting spoon** (brass or copper)
- **Tea light candle** (for melting wax)
- **Rigid gift box** (branded with LVR logo/aesthetic)
- **Instruction card** (beautifully designed, brand-aligned)
- **Optional: Branded cloth pouch or wrap**

## 3. Manufacturing Plan

**Phase 1: Sample & Test (10-20 units)**
- Order 10-20 sample kits to test quality
- Photograph for marketing
- Gift to influencers, past clients, friends
- Gather feedback on packaging, usability, aesthetic
- **Investment:** $150-$300
- **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks from order to delivery

**Phase 2: Micro Batch (30-50 units)**
- First paid production run
- Pre-orders or limited drop
- Test market demand
- Refine any quality issues from Phase 1
- **Investment:** $450-$1,100
- **Timeline:** 4-6 weeks

**Phase 3: Small Batch (100-150 units)**
- Scale only after proven demand from Phase 2
- Bulk pricing kicks in (lower per-unit cost)
- Can sell via website, wedding fairs, pop-ups
- **Investment:** $1,200-$3,300
- **Timeline:** 6-8 weeks

**Phase 4: Full Production (500+ units/year)**
- Only recommended after consistent demand
- Negotiate better supplier rates
- Consider U.S.-based assembly for faster fulfillment
- Potential wholesale to wedding boutiques
- **Investment:** $6,000-$11,000
- **Timeline:** Quarterly reorders

**⚠️ DO NOT manufacture 500 units upfront.** Start small, validate demand, scale gradually.

## 4. Supplier Sources

### Alibaba / AliExpress (Samples & Small Batches)
**Search strings to use:**
- `custom wax seal stamp`
- `engraved brass stamp head`
- `personalized wax seal kit wholesale`
- `wax seal set with box`
- `custom logo wax beads`
- `rigid box custom logo small quantity`

**Recommended suppliers to contact:**
- Look for suppliers with:
  - ✅ 4+ star rating
  - ✅ "Gold Supplier" badge
  - ✅ 50+ transactions
  - ✅ Accepts small MOQ (10-50 units)
  - ✅ Offers sample orders ($20-50)

**Typical lead times:**
- Sample: 7-14 days production + 10-20 days shipping
- Bulk order (50-100 units): 15-30 days production + shipping

### DHGate
- Similar to Alibaba but easier small-batch ordering
- Good for testing multiple suppliers quickly

### Etsy Wholesale Vendors (U.S.-Based Option)
- Search for sellers offering:
  - `custom wax seal engraving`
  - `bulk wax seal kits`
  - `private label sealing wax`

**Pros:** Faster shipping, easier communication, higher quality control
**Cons:** 2-3x more expensive than China suppliers

### Packaging Suppliers
- **Rigid boxes:** Search "custom rigid box small run" on Alibaba
- **Branded tissue paper:** Sticker Mule, Packhelp, Lumi
- **Instruction cards:** Print locally via Moo, VistaPrint, or local printer

## 9. Competitive Analysis

### Similar Products in Market

**1. Artisanal Wax Seal Kits on Etsy**
- Price range: $25-$80
- Quality varies widely
- Most are generic (initials, basic designs)
- **LVR Advantage:** Custom rose design, cohesive brand story, luxury positioning

**2. Wedding Stationery Brands**
- Minted, Papier, Artifact Uprising sell wax seal kits
- Price: $40-$100
- **LVR Advantage:** More personal, story-driven, not corporate

**3. Luxury Stationery Boutiques**
- Crane & Co, Smythson sell wax seal kits
- Price: $150-$300 (very high-end)
- **LVR Advantage:** More accessible price point, wedding-specific

### Market Gap
- **There's no wedding film brand selling luxury stationery items.**
- This positions LVR uniquely at the intersection of:
  - Cinematic storytelling
  - Tangible keepsakes
  - Luxury lifestyle branding

## 10. Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Low demand** | Medium | High | Start with 10-20 sample batch, pre-orders only |
| **Quality issues** | Medium | Medium | Order samples first, test thoroughly |
| **Shipping damage** | Low | Medium | Use rigid boxes, bubble wrap, insurance |
| **High inventory cost** | Low | High | Never order >50 units until demand proven |
| **Brand dilution** | Low | High | Keep design luxury, align with core brand |
| **Fulfillment time** | Medium | Low | Set clear shipping timelines (2-3 weeks) |
